Company Overview

The SunD water distillation unit has the ability to purify water thorough a fully energy efficient method without the necessity of additional infrastructure. With our patented technology our machine creates potable water regardless of the chemical and biological containments. The unit is the first of its kind that can be applied on a large scale while remaining profitable. Our solution will change the way the world approaches the water scarcity crisis.
