Clean Water For Everyone

The Problem

Lack of access to clean water is a global epidemic and will only continue to worsen with global population growth. These contaminants can cause a myriad of diseases, many of which can be fatal. These are all preventable with the installation of the SunD unit.

The Solution

We offer a cost-effective free-standing system for villages, communities, neighbourhoods or multi-family dwellings that currently lack other fresh water solutions.

Who We Are

The SunDwater founding and management team bring together the belief that collaboration between professionals can turn the tide and that change is possible.

FAQ's

How much does a SunD unit cost?

The cost of the unit depends on the quantity purchased. The cost of a single unit is approximately $16,000 (US).

What are the requirements needed to install a SunD unit?

The installation is simple and only requires a 15X15 cement pad.

How does humidity effect SunD’s efficiency?

The SunD’s efficiency increases with increased temperature and sunlight.

How long can the SunD can run uninterrupted?

As a result of its design we believe the unit can run for an almost unlimited amount of time

How much maintenance is required for the SunD?

Each unit requires minimal maintenance. Periodically, the heating oven will require cleaning of the residual debris. but this is also a simple process.

What happens if I need assistance with my unit?

SunD water will provide continued technical assistance, as required. Units can be monitored remotely.

Can purified water be stored?

Purified water can be stored in appropriate storage containers on site or at a water farm
